The game I am going to teach is Ironsworn! A game perilous quests by Shawn Tomkin. 2018 ENnie Award Winner for best free game. Ironsworn is a roleplaying game that doesn't require a game master. Just like life you, players make it up as they go. You decide how difficult you want the challenges to be. You decide if you've achieved your goal or not and what the consequences are.
Your goals are measured on Progress Tracks. Kill the dragon. Become a wizard. Convince the Circle of Elders to Tor is cool. You decide how hard each challenge is going to be setting a Challenge Rank:Troublesome, Dangerous, Formidable, Extreme, Epic.
The Challenge Rank determines how fast you complete the Progress Track.
When you think you've completed your goal you make *Progress Roll*. If you roll equal to or under the number of filled boxes, you've fulfilled your goal. Ironsworn is a semi-improvised storytelling game. If you don't make the roll, you decide what happens.
